Welcome to International Ministers Forum

Welcome to International Ministers Forum online and thank you for your interest in this ministry!

We are an Ordaining and Mission focused Christian organization and desire to literally reach the world for God with the demonstration of the power of the Holy Spirit. Our function in the Body of Christ is to support Christian Ministries/Missionaries with wise counsel, faith- and experienced-based direction/encouragement, Spiritual covering*, to be a valuable networking resource and to help promote unity and bring peace where there is division.

This is a day of importance for every one of us in the ministry. We are living in the end-time, therefore we must consider our actions and decisions.

The Scriptures advise us to make our calling and election sure, and to walk worthy of our vocation. Integrity, accountability and responsibility are very important to successful ministry.

Proverbs 11:14, "Where no counsel is, the people fall, but in the multitude of counselors there is safety."

Proverbs 15:22, "Without counsel purposes are disappointed; but in the multitude of counselors they are established."

To find out more about IMF, please download the Information Booklet or contact us.

* INTERNATIONAL MINISTERS FORUM is recognized by the Federal Government as an established Ecclesiastical Fellowship. It has been in existence since August 18, 1950.
